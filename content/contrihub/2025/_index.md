# ContriHub 2025

**October 15 - November 15, 2025**

ContriHub 2025 was our most successful open source celebration yet! Over a month, 45 participants from across years and branches made meaningful contributions to 8 repositories, resulting in 120+ merged pull requests.

## Event Overview

This year's ContriHub focused on improving our club's ecosystem - the website, DSA platform, alumni portal, and various utility tools. We also welcomed contributions to selected external projects.

### Theme: "Build What Matters"

We challenged participants to focus on contributions that create real value:
- Features that users actually need
- Bugs that affect daily usage
- Documentation that helps others learn
- Tests that improve reliability
