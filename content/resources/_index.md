# Learning Resources

A curated collection of the best learning materials, tools, and platforms to support your technical growth.

## üìö Learning Platforms

### Comprehensive Courses

**FreeCodeCamp** - [freecodecamp.org](https://www.freecodecamp.org/)  
Free, comprehensive web development curriculum with certificates.  
*Best for: Beginners, Web Development*

**The Odin Project** - [theodinproject.com](https://www.theodinproject.com/)  
Full-stack JavaScript or Ruby on Rails paths.  
*Best for: Self-paced learners, Full Stack*

**FullStackOpen** - [fullstackopen.com](https://fullstackopen.com/)  
University of Helsinki's modern web development course.  
*Best for: Intermediate learners, React/Node.js*

**CS50** - [cs50.harvard.edu](https://cs50.harvard.edu/)  
Harvard's introduction to computer science.  
*Best for: CS fundamentals*

## üíª Competitive Programming

### Practice Platforms

**LeetCode** - [leetcode.com](https://leetcode.com/)  
2000+ problems, company-specific questions, mock interviews.  
*Best for: Interview preparation*

**Codeforces** - [codeforces.com](https://codeforces.com/)  
Regular contests, rating system, editorial solutions.  
*Best for: Competitive programming*

**CodeChef** - [codechef.com](https://www.codechef.com/)  
Monthly contests, learning tracks, peer discussion.  
*Best for: Beginners to advanced*

**AtCoder** - [atcoder.jp](https://atcoder.jp/)  
Japanese platform, high-quality problems.  
*Best for: Advanced algorithmic challenges*

**HackerRank** - [hackerrank.com](https://www.hackerrank.com/)  
Skill certifications, domain-specific tracks.  
*Best for: Structured learning*
